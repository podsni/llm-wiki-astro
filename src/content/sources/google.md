# Google

## Profil

**Google** adalah perusahaan teknologi multinasional AS, anak perusahaan **Alphabet Inc.** Didirikan 1998 oleh Larry Page dan Sergey Brin saat PhD di Stanford. Awalnya search engine, sekarang berbagai produk.

## Produk Utama

- **Search** (90%+ market share)
- **YouTube** (2,5 miliar user)
- **Android** (70%+ mobile OS)
- **Chrome** — browser
- **Workspace** (Gmail, Docs, Drive)
- **Cloud** (GCP)
- **Maps**
- **Play Store**
- **Pixel** (smartphone)

## AI

- **Google AI** (gabungan Brain + DeepMind)
- **Gemini** — LLM multimodal
- **Veo** — text-to-video
- **Imagen** — text-to-image
- **TensorFlow, JAX, Keras** — frameworks
- **TPU** — custom AI chip
- **NotebookLM** — research assistant
- **Project Astra** — universal AI

## Alphabet Subsidiaries

- **Google** (search, ads, cloud)
- **Waymo** (self-driving)
- **DeepMind** (AI research)
- **Verily** (life sciences)
- **Calico** (longevity)
- **Wing** (drones)
- **Loon** (internet balloons - shut down)

## Pendiri

- **Larry Page** (co-founder, sampai 2019 CEO Alphabet)
- **Sergey Brin** (co-founder, President Alphabet)
- **Sundar Pichai** (CEO Google, 2015-)
