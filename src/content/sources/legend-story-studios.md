# Legend Story Studios

## Profil

**Legend Story Studios (LSS)** adalah studio TCG asal **Selandia Baru**, didirikan 2019. Produsen **Flesh and Blood TCG**.

## Sejarah

- **2019** — Didirikan di Auckland
- **2019** — Welcome to Rathe (set 1)
- **2020** — Arcane Rising
- **2021** — Crucible of War, Tales of Aria
- **2022** — Uprising
- **2023** — Dusk Till Dawn, Outsiders, Bright Lights, Heavy Hitters
- **2024** — High Seas, Aether Irregulars

## Pendiri

- **James White** — CEO
- **Alexander Chiap** — co-founder
- **Richard Garfield** — game designer (bergabung 2020)

## Produk

- **Flesh and Blood TCG** — flagship
- **Rad Lands** (2021) — game kartu khusus
- **Tales of Aria: Young Hero** (2023) — game board
- **Everfest** (2021) — event digital

## Pasar

- Selandia Baru (kantor pusat)
- Australia
- Amerika
- Eropa
- Asia
- Pertumbuhan cepat 2023-2024
- $100M+ revenue (2024)
