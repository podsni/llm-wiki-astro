# Anthropic

## Profil

**Anthropic** adalah perusahaan AI asal **San Francisco, California** yang fokus pada **AI safety**. Pembuat model **Claude** dan pemimpin agentic AI 2025-2026.

## Sejarah

- **2021** — didirikan oleh Dario Amodei & Daniela Amodei, mantan VP Research & VP People di OpenAI
- **2022** — Claude 1 (private beta)
- **2023** — Claude 1 (public), Claude 2
- **2024** — Claude 3 (Haiku, Sonnet, Opus), Claude 3.5 Sonnet
- **2024** — Computer Use (agentic AI pioneer)
- **2025** — Claude 4, 4.5 Sonnet, Claude Code
- **2025** — valuation $200B+, ARR $5B+
- **2026** — Frontier agents, mechanistic interpretability breakthroughs

## Produk

- **Claude** (chatbot & API)
- **Claude Code** (agentic coding di terminal)
- **Computer Use** (browser & OS control)
- **MCP** (Model Context Protocol — standar terbuka)
- **API**

## Pendanaan

- **2022** — $580M Series B
- **2023** — $1.25B (Google, Salesforce, Amazon)
- **2024** — $4B (Amazon $4B, total $8B Amazon)
- **2025** — $2B (Lightspeed, Bain)
- **Valuation**: $60B (awal 2025), $200B+ (akhir 2025)

## Konsep Kunci

- **Constitutional AI** — alignment dengan prinsip
- **Mech-Interp** — mechanistic interpretability
- **Responsible Scaling Policy** — safety framework
- **AI welfare** — apakah AI consciousness harus dipertimbangkan

## Klien Enterprise

Bank of America, Pfizer, Deloitte, GitLab, Slack, Zoom, Bridgewater, dll.
