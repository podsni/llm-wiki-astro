# Allen Newell

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 19 Maret 1927, San Francisco
- **Meninggal**: 19 Juli 1992, Pittsburgh (usia 65)
- **Profesi**: Cognitive scientist, AI researcher

## Kontribusi

- **Logic Theorist** (1956, dengan Simon) — program AI pertama
- **General Problem Solver (GPS)** (1957)
- **Soar** (1986) — cognitive architecture dengan John Laird
- **Unified Theories of Cognition** (1990)

## Penghargaan

- **Turing Award** (1975) — dengan Herbert Simon
- **National Medal of Science** (1992)
- **Research Award AI** (1979)
