# David Rumelhart

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 12 Juni 1942, Mitchell, South Dakota
- **Meninggal**: 13 Maret 2011, Chelsea, Michigan
- **Profesi**: Cognitive scientist

## Kontribusi

- **Backpropagation** (1986) — paper seminal dengan Hinton & Williams
- **Parallel Distributed Processing (PDP)** (1986) — buku dengan James McClelland
- **Generalized delta rule**
- **Distributed representations**

## Buku

- *Parallel Distributed Processing: Explorations in the Microstructure of Cognition* (1986) — 2 volume dengan McClelland

## Karier

- **UCSD** — professor (1962-1987)
- **Stanford** — professor (1987-1998)
- **University of Michigan** (1998-2011)

## Legasi

Rumelhart adalah **arsitek utama revolusi neural network** 1986. PDP book menjadi textbook standar, backpropagation menjadi fondasi training neural network.
