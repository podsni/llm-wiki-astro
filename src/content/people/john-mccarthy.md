# John McCarthy

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 4 September 1927, Boston, AS
- **Meninggal**: 24 Oktober 2011, Stanford, California (usia 84)
- **Profesi**: Ilmuwan komputer

## Kontribusi

- **Istilah "Artificial Intelligence"** (1955, proposal Dartmouth)
- **Konfrensi Dartmouth 1956** — pendiri sekaligus co-organizer
- **LISP** (1958) — bahasa pemrograman AI dominan
- **Advice Taker** (1958) — konsep knowledge representation
- **Situational Calculus** — formalisasi aksi dan perubahan
- **Garbage collection** — konsep di LISP
- **Time-sharing system** (1960-an)

## Karier

- **Dartmouth College** (1955–1958)
- **MIT** (1958–1962)
- **Stanford** (1962–2000) — founder SAIL (Stanford AI Lab)
- **Turing Award** (1971)

## Kutipan Terkenal

> *"As soon as it works, no one calls it AI anymore."*
