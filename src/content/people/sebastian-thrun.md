# Sebastian Thrun

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 14 Mei 1967, Solingen, Jerman
- **Profesi**: Roboticist, educator, entrepreneur

## Kontribusi

- **Stanley** (2005) — mobil otonom yang memenangkan **DARPA Grand Challenge** di gurun Mojave
- **Google Self-Driving Car** (2009) — sekarang **Waymo** —副总裁
- **Google X** —副总裁
- **Udacity** (2012) — MOOC untuk tech
- **Kitty Hawk** (2018) — flying car
- **Stellar** (2022) — pendidikan AI

## Karier

- **Carnegie Mellon**, **Stanford** (1995-2011) — professor
- **Google** (2011-2014) —副总裁
- **Udacity** (2012-)
- **Kitty Hawk** (2018-)

- **DARPA Grand Challenge winner** (2005)

## Legacy

Thrun memicu **industri self-driving modern** — Stanley (2005) membuktikan bahwa mobil otonom bisa di lapangan terbuka, dan Google Self-Driving Project (2009) memimpin industri.
