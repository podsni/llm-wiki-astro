# Gary Marcus

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 1970
- **Profesi**: Scientist, author, entrepreneur

## Pandangan

- **Skeptis** bahwa LLM bisa mencapai AGI
- Mendorong **hybrid neurosymbolic** AI — gabungkan neural network + symbolic reasoning
- Kritikus keras **hype** AI

## Buku

- *The Algebraic Mind* (2001)
- *Kluge* (2008)
- *Guitar Zero* (2012)
- *Rebooting AI* (2019, dengan Ernest Davis)
- *Taming Silicon Valley** (2024)

## Karier

- **NYU** — Professor of Psychology & Neural Science
- **Geometric Intelligence** (2014) — co-founder (dijual Uber 2016)
- **Robust.AI** (2019) — co-founder & CEO
- **Wait But Why** essays populer

## Argumen

Marcus argumen:
- LLM **tidak memiliki model dunia** (world model)
- LLM **gagal pada compositional reasoning**
- LLM **gampang di-fool** dengan adversarial example
- Kita butuh **representasi simbolik eksplisit** + learning
