# Dai Vernon

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 11 Juni 1894, Ottawa, Kanada
- **Meninggal**: 21 Agustus 1992, Martinsville, New Jersey
- **Profesi**: Magician, penulis, card manipulator

## Kontribusi

- **The Professor** — julukan
- **The Dai Vernon Book of Magic** (1957)
- **Dai Vernon's Tribute to Houdini** (1959)
- **The Dai Vernon Anthology** (1993)
- **Trilogi Tribute** — Mystery, Escamotage, Silvester

## Trik Ikonik

- **Torn and Restored Card**
- **Ambitious Card**
- **Brainwave**
- **Wild Card**
- **The Four Burglars**

## Magic Circle

- **1938** — Magician of the Year
- **FISM** (International Federation of Magic Societies) — special award
- **First Canadian** di FISM Grand Prix

## Pengaruh

- Mentor **Ricky Jay**, **Michael Close**
- Konsultan **Dai Vernon** di **Theory11** (brand)
- Daftar anggota **The Magic Circle** (London)
- Generasi card magician modern

## Legacy

- Card magic modern banyak berutang padanya
- "Move toward naturalness" — trik yang bersih
- **The Vernon Touch** — natural handling
