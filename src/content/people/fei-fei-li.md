# Fei-Fei Li

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 1976, Beijing, China (naturalized US citizen)
- **Profesi**: AI researcher, educator

## Kontribusi

- **ImageNet** (2009) — 14 juta+ labeled images, 20.000+ kategori
  - Memicu **ImageNet Challenge** yang dimenangkan AlexNet 2012
  - Trigger revolusi deep learning
- **Stanford AI Lab** — director
- **Stanford HAI** (Human-Centered AI Institute) — co-director (2019)
- **AI4ALL** (2017) — NGO untuk demokratisasi AI
- **World Labs** (2024) — spatial intelligence, valuation >$1B

## Buku

- *The Worlds I See* (2023) — memoir

## Pandangan

- **Human-centered AI** — AI harus melayani manusia
- **Spatial intelligence** (3D understanding) — next frontier setelah LLM
- Mendukung **AI diversity** — lebih banyak underrepresented groups di AI
