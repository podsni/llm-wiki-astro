# Shane Legg

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 1973
- **Profesi**: AI researcher

## Kontribusi

- **Co-founder DeepMind** (2010, dengan Hassabis & Suleyman)
- **Chief AGI Scientist** di Google DeepMind
- **Definisi AGI** — *'the capacity to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks at human-level or beyond'*

## Karier

- **University of Waikato** (PhD)
- **DeepMind** (2010) — co-founder
- **Google DeepMind** (2014–)

## Pandangan

- **AGI mungkin dalam dekade ini** (2030-an)
- **DeepMind** fokus pada **responsible AGI**
