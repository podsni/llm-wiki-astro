# Peter Adkison

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 1961
- **Profesi**: Entrepreneur, game designer

## Kontribusi

- **Wizards of the Coast** (1990) — co-founder (dengan Richard Garfield)
- **Magic: The Gathering** (1993) — published
- **Dungeons & Dragons 3rd Edition** (2000) — acquired TSR
- **Gen Con** — acquired 1997 (largest tabletop gaming convention)
- **Avalon Hill** — acquired 1999
- **Hasbro acquired WotC** — 1997

## Karier

- **University of Washington** (Math, Computer Science)
- **1990** — Wizards of the Coast
- **1997** — Hasbro acquisition ($325M)
- **1999** — Pensiun dari WotC
- **2010s+** — CEO **Hex Kit** (board game publishing platform)

## Visi

- **Demokratisasi game** — game untuk semua orang
- **Standar tinggi** untuk produksi
- **Komunitas** yang inklusif

## Legacy

- Magic: The Gathering — warisan abadi
- D&D edisi 5 — bring D&D ke era modern
- Gen Con — komunitas tabletop gaming
