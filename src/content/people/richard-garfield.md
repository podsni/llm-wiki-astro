# Richard Garfield

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 26 Juni 1963, Boston, AS
- **Profesi**: Matematikawan, game designer

## Kontribusi

- **Magic: The Gathering** (1993) — TCG pertama
- **RoboRally** (1994) — board game
- **The Great Dalmuti** (1995) — card game
- **Star Wars Trading Card Game** (2002)
- **KeyForge** (2018)
- **King of Tokyo** (2011)
- **Rad Lands** (2021) — Legend Story Studios

## Sejarah

- **PhD Matematika** dari University of Pennsylvania
- Bekerja sebagai matematikawan di University of Washington
- **1991** — Wawancara Peter Adkison (Wizards of the Coast) tentang game baru
- **1993** — Magic: The Gathering rilis
- **2020** — Bergabung Legend Story Studios (Flesh and Blood)
- **2023** — Membuat *Rad Lands*, *Tales of Aria: Young Hero*

## Karier Sekarang

- **Legend Story Studios** (Flesh and Blood, Rad Lands)
- Konsultan design untuk banyak TCG
- Game design workshop

## Penghargaan

- **Inkpot Award** (1994) — San Diego Comic-Con
- **Diana Jones Award** (2007)
- **Games Magazine** Game of the Year (1994)
