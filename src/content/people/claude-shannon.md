# Claude Shannon

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 30 April 1916, Petoskey, Michigan
- **Meninggal**: 24 Februari 2001, Medford, Massachusetts (usia 84)
- **Profesi**: Matematikawan, electrical engineer, cryptographer

## Kontribusi

- **Tesis Master (1937)** — *"A Symbolic Analysis of Relay and Switching Circuits"* — Boolean algebra untuk sirkuit listrik, fondasi desain komputer digital
- **Teori Informasi (1948)** — *A Mathematical Theory of Communication* — konsep bit, entropy, channel capacity
- **Catur komputer** — *Programming a Computer for Playing Chess* (1950)
- **Theseus mouse** (1950) — maze-solving mouse, magnetic
- **Shannon number** — batas bawah game tree catur (10^120)
- **Juggling robot** (1950-an)
- **Unicyclist** — hobi seumur hidup

## Karier

- **Bell Labs** (1941–1972)
- **MIT** (1956–1978)

## Penghargaan

- **National Medal of Science** (1966)
- **IEEE Medal of Honor** (1966)
- **Turing Award** (1985)
