# Hans Moravec

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 30 November 1948, Klagenfurt, Austria
- **Profesi**: Robotikawan, futurolog

## Kontribusi

- **Stanford Cart** (1979-1980) — kendaraan otonom pertama (5 menit per meter)
- **CMU Robotics Institute** — co-founder
- **Moravec's paradox** (1988) — *it's comparatively easy to make computers exhibit adult-level performance on intelligence tests or playing checkers, and difficult or impossible to give them the skills of a one-year-old when it comes to perception and mobility*

## Buku

- *Mind Children: The Future of Robot and Human Intelligence* (1988)
- *Robot: Mere Machine to Transcendent Mind* (1999)
- *Surviving AI* (2015)

## Futurologi

- **Transhumanisme** — manusia akan menjadi robot/AI
- **Mind uploading** — consciousness dapat ditransfer
- **Prediction** (1999): AI akan mencapai human-level pada 2040
