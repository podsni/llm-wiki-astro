# Kazuki Takahashi

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 4 Oktober 1961, Tokyo
- **Meninggal**: 6 Juli 2022 (usia 60)
- **Profesi**: Mangaka, game designer

- **Yu-Gi-Oh!** manga (1996-2004) — Shonen Jump
- **Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters** anime
- **Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game** (1999) — bersama Konami
- **Yu-Gi-Oh! GX, 5D's, Zexal, Arc-V, VRAINS**
