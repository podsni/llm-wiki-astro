# Andrew Ng

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 18 April 1976, London (orang tua dari Hong Kong)
- **Profesi**: AI/ML researcher, educator, entrepreneur

## Karier

- **Stanford** — Associate Professor, CS229 (ML course paling populer)
- **Google Brain** (2011) — co-founder (dengan Jeff Dean)
- **Baidu** (2014–2017) — Chief Scientist
- **Coursera** (2012) — co-founder
- **DeepLearning.AI** (2017) — founder
- **LandingAI** (2017) — founder, computer vision industri
- **AI Fund** (2017) — venture fund

## Kontribusi

- **Stanford CS229** — ribuan siswa
- **Coursera Machine Learning** course — 5 juta+ siswa
- **Deep Learning Specialization** — Coursera
- **The Batch** — newsletter populer
- **MADI** (Massive AI Deployment Initiative) — $120M untuk adopsi AI

## Pandangan

- **AI for everyone** — demokratisasi AI
- **Data-centric AI** — fokus pada data, bukan model
- **AI tidak akan menggantikan pekerjaan, tapi orang yang pakai AI akan menggantikan orang yang tidak**
