# Demis Hassabis

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 27 Juli 1976, London
- **Profesi**: AI researcher, neuroscientist, game designer

## Kontribusi

- **DeepMind** (2010) — pendiri, di-akuisisi Google 2014
- **AlphaGo** (2016) — kalahkan Lee Sedol 4-1
- **AlphaZero** (2017) — belajar dari nol, master catur/go/shogi
- **AlphaFold** (2018–2020) — selesaikan protein folding problem
- **Gemini** (2023+) — model frontier Google
- **Veo** (2024) — text-to-video

## Penghargaan

- **Nobel Prize in Chemistry 2024** — bersama John Jumper, untuk AlphaFold
- **CBE** (2024)
- **Time 100** paling berpengaruh

## Catur

- **Chess Master** usia 13 (2300+ ELO)
- **Mind Sports Olympiad** multiple winner
- **Pentamind** winner (5 different games)
