# Jensen Huang

## Biodata

- **Lahir**: 17 Februari 1963, Tainan, Taiwan
- **Profesi**: CEO, co-founder NVIDIA

## Karier

- **AMD**, **LSI Logic** (1980-an)
- **NVIDIA** (1993) — co-founder, CEO
- **Stanford** (MS EE)

## Kontribusi

- **GeForce** (1999) — GPU gaming
- **CUDA** (2006) — GPU computing
- **AI GPU**: K40, P100, V100, A100, H100, B100, B200
- **Tren AI**: NVIDIA's CUDA + GPU menjadi standar de facto

## Valuasi

- **2020** — $300B
- **2023** — $1T (pertama kali, awal AI boom)
- **2024** — $3T+ (terbesar di dunia)
- **2025** — fluktuasi $2.5T-$3.5T

## Produk AI Penting

- **A100** (2020) — standard LLM training 2021-2023
- **H100** (2022) — Transformer revolution
- **H200** (2023)
- **B100, B200** (2024) — Blackwell
- **GB200 NVL72** (2024) — rack-scale

## Kutipan Terkenal

- *"We are at the iPhone moment of AI"*
- *"Buy more GPUs"*
