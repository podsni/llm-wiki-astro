# Sejarah Komputer

## Definisi

**Komputer** adalah mesin yang dapat diprogram untuk melakukan komputasi, menyimpan, dan memproses data. Dari sempoa sederhana hingga AI superkomputer.

## Era

### 1. Kalkulator Mekanik (1642-1900)
- **Blaise Pascal** — Pascaline (1642)
- **Gottfried Leibniz** — Stepped Reckoner (1694)
- **Charles Babbage** — Difference Engine (1822), Analytical Engine (1837)
- **Ada Lovelace** — programmer pertama (1843)
- **Herman Hollerith** — tabulating machine (1890) → IBM

### 2. Komputer Elektronik Awal (1940-1955)
- **ENIAC** (1945) — 18.000 tabung vakum, 30 ton
- **EDVAC, UNIVAC I** (1951)
- **John von Neumann** — arsitektur stored-program (1945)
- **Alan Turing** — Turing Machine (1936), Colossus (1943)

### 3. Transistor & Integrated Circuit (1947-1970)
- **Transistor** di Bell Labs (1947) — Bardeen, Brattain, Shockley
- **Integrated circuit** — Kilby (Texas Instruments) 1958, Noyce (Fairchild) 1959
- **IBM 360** (1964) — mainframe standar
- **DEC PDP-8** (1965) — minicomputer

### 4. Personal Computer (1971-1995)
- **Intel 4004** (1971) — first microprocessor
- **Apple II** (1977)
- **IBM PC** (1981)
- **Macintosh** (1984) — GUI
- **Windows 3.1** (1992) — mainstream GUI

### 5. Internet & Multimedia (1995-2010)
- **World Wide Web** (1991)
- **Google** (1998)
- **Broadband** (2000+)
- **YouTube** (2005)
- **Smartphone**: iPhone (2007)

### 6. Mobile & Cloud (2010-2020)
- **App economy**
- **AWS** (2006+)
- **SaaS** (Salesforce, Google Docs)
- **4G, 5G**
- **AI/ML** mainstream (2012+)

### 7. AI & Quantum (2020+)
- **Deep learning** mainstream
- **LLM** (2020+)
- **Quantum computing**: IBM Q, Google Sycamore
- **AI accelerators**: GPU, TPU, NPU

## Generasi

1. **Vacuum tubes** (1940-1955)
2. **Transistors** (1955-1965)
3. **Integrated circuits** (1965-1980)
4. **Microprocessors** (1980+)
5. **AI/Quantum** (2020+)
