# Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG

## Definisi

**Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG** adalah trading card game yang diadaptasi dari manga/anime Yu-Gi-Oh! karya Kazuki Takahashi. Diproduksi oleh **Konami** sejak 1999.

## Sejarah

- **1996** — Manga Yu-Gi-Oh! dimulai (Shonen Jump)
- **1998** — *Yu-Gi-Oh!* anime
- **1999** — Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG (Konami, Jepang)
- **2002** — Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters anime
- **2008** — Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's
- **2023** — Yu-Gi-Oh! GO RUSH

## Game

- **40-60 kartu deck**
- **Main Deck, Extra Deck, Side Deck**
- **Summon types**:
  - **Normal** — 1 monster per turn
  - **Tribute/Sacrifice** — level 5-6+
  - **Ritual** — kartu Ritual Spell
  - **Fusion** — 2+ monster + Fusion Spell
  - **Synchro** — 1 Tuner + 1+
  - **Xyz** — 2+ level sama
  - **Pendulum** — di Spell/Trap zone
  - **Link** — 1+ Link monster
- **Speed meta** — banyak deck hanya butuh 1-2 turn

## Format

- **OCG** (Asia/Jepang) — beberapa set lebih awal
- **TCG** (selain Asia)
- **Forbidden/Limited List** — kartu yang dibatasi

## Kartu Ikonik

- **Exodia the Forbidden One** — 5 kartu, instant win
- **Blue-Eyes White Dragon** — kartu legendaris
- **Dark Magician** — kartu karakter
- **Black Luster Soldier** — kartu termahal ($120K+ Tournament Black Luster Soldier 1999)
