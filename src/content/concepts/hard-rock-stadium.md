# Hard Rock Stadium

## Profil

**Hard Rock Stadium** di **Miami Gardens, Florida**, AS. Home of Miami Dolphins (NFL), Miami Hurricanes, dan venue Miami Open (tenis).

## Sejarah

- **1987** — Dibuka sebagai Joe Robbie Stadium
- **1990** — Super Bowl XXIII
- **2005** — Berganti nama Land Shark Stadium
- **2016** — Hard Rock Stadium
- **2020** — Super Bowl LIV
- **2026** — Venue World Cup

## Kapasitas

- **65.000** (NFL)
- **75.000** (konser, World Cup)
- **Tennis**: 30.000

## Penggunaan

- **NFL**: Miami Dolphins
- **NCAA**: Miami Hurricanes, Orange Bowl
- **Tenis**: Miami Open (WTA, ATP)
- **F1**: Miami Grand Prix
- **NASCAR**: Ford Championship Weekend
