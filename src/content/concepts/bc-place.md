# BC Place

## Profil

**BC Place** di **Vancouver, British Columbia**, Kanada. Home of **Vancouver Whitecaps** (MLS) dan **BC Lions** (CFL). Retractable roof. Venue **2010 Winter Olympics** dan **2015 FIFA Women's World Cup Final**.

## Sejarah

- **1983** — Dibuka
- **2010** — Winter Olympics (opening, closing, ice hockey)
- **2015** — FIFA Women's World Cup Final (USA 5-2 Jepang)
- **2018** — Atap dimodernisasi
- **2026** — Venue World Cup

## Kapasitas

- **54.000** (reguler)
- **59.000** (concert, World Cup)

## Fitur

- **Retractable roof** — Teflon-coated fiberglass
- **Largest cable-supported roof** in the world

## Penggunaan

- **MLS**: Vancouver Whitecaps FC
- **CFL**: BC Lions
- **Canada Soccer**: timnas
- **NCAA**: Exhibition games
