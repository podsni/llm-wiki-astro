# Disney Lorcana

## Definisi

**Disney Lorcana** adalah trading card game oleh **Ravensburger** (2023) dengan karakter-karakter Disney (Mickey, Elsa, Stitch, dll) sebagai 'glimmer' yang berjuang di 'Realm of Lorcana'.

## Sejarah

- **2022** — Diumumkan (D23 Expo)
- **2023** — The First Chapter (set 1)
- **2023** — Rise of the Floodborn
- **2024** — Into the Inklands, Ursula's Return

## Game

- **60-kartu deck** (minimum)
- **6 warna** (Ink): Amber, Amethyst, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, Steel
- **Lore** — bermain sebagai Illumineer, melawan flood
- **Mechanic**: Songs, Actions, Items, Locations, Characters

## Format

- **Core Constructed** — semua set
- **Limited (Sealed, Draft)**

## Karakter Ikonik

- **Mickey Mouse, Brave Little Tailor** (Amber)
- **Maleficent, Sinister Visitor** (Amethyst)
- **Stitch, Rock Star** (Ruby)
- **Elsa, Spirit of Winter** (Sapphire)
- **Donald Duck, Boisterous Fowl** (Emerald)
- **Captain Hook, Forceful Duelist** (Steel)

## Pasar

- **$200M+ revenue** (2023-2024)
- **Lonjakan harga** (Mickey, Stitch Enchanted)
- **Mickey Mouse, Brave Little Tailor (Enchanted)** — $5K+
- **Fast adoption** di 2023
