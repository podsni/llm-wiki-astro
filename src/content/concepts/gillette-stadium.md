# Gillette Stadium

## Profil

**Gillette Stadium** di **Foxborough, Massachusetts**, AS. Home of New England Patriots (NFL) dan New England Revolution (MLS). Pertama kali menampung MLS (1996) bersama NFL.

## Sejarah

- **2002** — Dibuka
- **2002** — Super Bowl XXXVI
- **2004** — Super Bowl XXXIX
- **2015** — Super Bowl XLIX
- **2017** — Super Bowl LI (Patriots comeback)
- **2019** — Super Bowl LIII
- **2026** — Venue World Cup

## Kapasitas

- **65.000** (NFL)
- **68.000** (konser)

## Penggunaan

- **NFL**: New England Patriots
- **MLS**: New England Revolution
- **NCAA**: Boston College, Army-Navy (2018)
- **Konser**: Taylor Swift, Ed Sheeran
