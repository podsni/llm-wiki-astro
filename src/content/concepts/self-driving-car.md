# Self-Driving Car

## Definisi

**Self-driving car** (mobil otonom) adalah kendaraan yang dapat **merasakan lingkungan** dan **bergerak dengan aman** tanpa intervensi manusia.

## SAE Level

- **Level 0** — Tanpa otomatisasi
- **Level 1** — Driver assistance (cruise control, lane keeping)
- **Level 2** — Partial automation (Tesla Autopilot, GM Super Cruise)
- **Level 3** — Conditional automation (Audi A8 2017, Honda Legend 2021)
- **Level 4** — High automation (Waymo, Cruise di area terbatas)
- **Level 5** — Full automation (belum ada)

## Tonggak

- **1986** — Ernst Dickmanns, Mercedes, van otonom di jalan tol
- **2004** — DARPA Grand Challenge (tidak ada finis)
- **2005** — Stanley (Stanford) menang DARPA Grand Challenge
- **2007** — DARPA Urban Challenge
- **2009** — Google Self-Driving (Waymo)
- **2015** — Tesla Autopilot
- **2020** — Waymo One (robotaxi komersial)
- **2024** — Waymo One di 5+ kota AS
