# Natural Language Processing

## Definisi

**NLP** adalah sub-bidang AI yang menangani **pemahaman, generasi, dan interaksi** dengan bahasa manusia (alami).

## Tugas

- **Tokenization, stemming, lemmatization**
- **POS tagging, NER**
- **Sentiment analysis**
- **Machine translation**
- **Question answering**
- **Summarization**
- **Text generation**

## Era

1. **Rule-based** (1950–1990) — hand-crafted rules
2. **Statistical** (1990–2014) — HMM, CRF, n-grams
3. **Neural** (2014–2017) — word2vec, RNN, LSTM
4. **Transformer** (2017–sekarang) — BERT, GPT
