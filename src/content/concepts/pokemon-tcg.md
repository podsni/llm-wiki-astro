# Pokémon TCG

## Definisi

**Pokémon Trading Card Game** adalah TCG berdasarkan franchise Pokémon. Dimulai di Jepang 1996, menyebar ke seluruh dunia. Industri terbesar kedua setelah Magic.

## Sejarah

- **1996** — Game Boy, kartu di Jepang (MediaFactory)
- **1998** — Pokémon USA (Wizards of the Coast)
- **1999** — Base Set di AS (B&W)
- **2003** — The Pokémon Company
- **2016** — Pokémon TCG Online (digital)
- **2019** — Pokémon TCG Live (digital)
- **2024** — lebih dari 16 miliar kartu dicetak

## Game

- **60-kartu deck**
- **Energi**: Grass, Fire, Water, Lightning, Psychic, Fighting, Darkness, Metal, Dragon, Fairy
- **Evolusi**: Basic → Stage 1 → Stage 2
- **Trainer**: Item, Supporter, Stadium
- **Format**: Standard, Expanded, Legacy (rotating)

## Set Penting

- **Base Set, Jungle, Fossil** (1998-1999)
- **Neo Genesis, Neo Discovery** (2000-2001)
- **EX Series** (2003-2007)
- **Diamond & Pearl** (2007-2009)
- **Black & White** (2011-2013)
- **XY** (2013-2016)
- **Sun & Moon** (2017-2019)
- **Sword & Shield** (2020-2022)
- **Scarlet & Violet** (2023+)

## Kartu Termahal

- **Pikachu Illustrator** (1998) — **$5.275M** (Goldin, 2022)
- **1999 1st Edition Holographic Charizard** — $420K+
- **Trophy Pikachu Gold** (japan) — $200K+
- **Prerelease Raichu** (1999) — $50K+

- **Regional, National, International**
- **Pokémon TCG Live** (digital competitive)
- **Liga Pokémon** (esports league)
