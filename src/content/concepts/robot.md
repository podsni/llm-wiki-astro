# Robot

## Definisi

**Robot** adalah **mesin fisik** yang dapat melakukan tugas secara otonom atau semi-otonom, biasanya dengan kemampuan sensing, processing, dan actuation.

## Komponen

- **Sensor** (cameras, LIDAR, IMU, force)
- **Actuator** (motors, pneumatics, hydraulics)
- **Controller** (microcontroller, computer)
- **Software** (perception, planning, control)

## Jenis

- **Industrial** — Unimate, FANUC, KUKA, ABB
- **Service** — Roomba (vacuum), Aibo (dog)
- **Humanoid** — Optimus (Tesla), Figure 01, Atlas (Boston Dynamics), H1 (Unitree)
- **Medical** — Da Vinci (surgery)
- **Military** — drones, bomb disposal
- **Agricultural** — John Deere, harvesting
- **Space** — Mars rovers (Curiosity, Perseverance)
- **Domestic** — Roomba, lawn mower

## Era Modern

- **2020s** — embodied AI, foundation models for robotics
- **2023+** — humanoids mulai massal (Optimus, Figure)
- **2024+** — autonomous robots di warehouse (Amazon, Symbotic)
- **2025-2026** — embodied AI agents (RT-2, π0)
