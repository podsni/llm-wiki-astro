# AT&T Stadium

## Profil

**AT&T Stadium** (sebelumnya *Cowboys Stadium*) di **Arlington, Texas**, AS. Home of Dallas Cowboys (NFL). Kapasitas 80.000-100.000+. Video board terbesar di dunia.

## Sejarah

- **2009** — Dibuka
- **2013** — Berganti nama jadi AT&T
- **2014** — NCAA Final Four
- **2015** — College Football Playoff Championship
- **2026** — Venue World Cup

## Kapasitas

- **80.000** (konfigurasi reguler)
- **100.000+** (standing room, konser)
- **Rekor**: 105.121 (NBA All-Star 2010)

## Video Board

- **160 ft × 72 ft** (49 m × 22 m)
- **Resolusi 4K HD**
- **Luas** 11.520 sq ft
- **Terbesar di dunia** untuk indoor venue

## Penggunaan

- **NFL**: Dallas Cowboys
- **NCAA**: Cotton Bowl, Final Four
- **NCAA Basketball**: 2014 Final Four
- **Wrestling**: WrestleMania 32
- **Konser**: Beyonce, Taylor Swift, Ed Sheeran
