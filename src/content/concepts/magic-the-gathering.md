# Magic: The Gathering

## Definisi

**Magic: The Gathering (MTG)** adalah **trading card game pertama** di dunia, diciptakan oleh [Richard Garfield](/people/richard-garfield) pada **1993** dan diproduksi oleh **Wizards of the Coast**. Industri terbesar di antara TCG.

## Aturan Dasar

- **60-kartu deck** (minimum)
- **5 warna** (White, Blue, Black, Red, Green) — *WUBRG*
- **Mana** — sumber daya untuk cast spell
- **Planeswalker** — kartu karakter yang kuat
- **Creature, Instant, Sorcery, Enchantment, Artifact, Land, Planeswalker**

## 5 Warna dan Filosofi

- **White (W)** — keadilan, komunitas, healing
- **Blue (U)** — knowledge, manipulation
- **Black (B)** — power, death, ambition
- **Red (R)** — passion, chaos, fire
- **Green (G)** — nature, growth, instinct

## Format

- **Standard** — set 1-3 tahun terakhir
- **Modern** — 2003+
- **Legacy** — semua set, beberapa banned
- **Vintage** — semua set, hampir semua legal
- **Commander (EDH)** — 100-kartu singleton
- **Pauper** — hanya Common
- **Limited (Draft, Sealed)**

## Set Penting

- **Alpha, Beta, Unlimited** (1993) — kartu paling langka
- **Arabian Nights, Antiquities, Legends, The Dark** (1993-1994)
- **Ravnica, Mirrodin, Zendikar, Innistrad** (block popular)
- **Core Set** series

## Kartu Paling Berharga

- **Black Lotus** (1993) — $500K+
- **Mox Sapphire, Jet, Emerald, Ruby, Pearl** (1993) — ratusan ribu
- **Time Walk** (1993) — $200K+
- **One Ring** (2023) — $2.6M (lelang Christie's 2024)
- **Tropical Island** (Revised, 1994) — $20K

## Turnamen

- **World Championship** (Wizards of the Coast)
- **Pro Tour** (1996+)
- **Mythic Championship**
- **MTG Arena Qualifier**
- **SCG Tour**
- **Lokal**: FNM (Friday Night Magic)

## Sejarah

- **1993** — Richard Garfield menciptakan
- **1993** — Alpha, Beta, Unlimited
- **1996** — Pro Tour dimulai
- **1997** — Wizards of the Coast acquired by Hasbro
- **2018** — MTG Arena (digital)
- **2023** — Magic Universes Beyond (crossover: LotR, Fallout, Final Fantasy)
- **2024** — 30th Anniversary
