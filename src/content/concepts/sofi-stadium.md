# SoFi Stadium

## Profil

**SoFi Stadium** di **Inglewood, California**, AS. Home of LA Rams, LA Chargers (NFL). Venue **Super Bowl LVI (2022)**, **College Football Playoff Championship 2023**, dan **2028 LA Olympics**.

## Sejarah

- **2020** — Dibuka ($5.5B, paling mahal di dunia)
- **2022** — Super Bowl LVI
- **2022** — NFL Rams juara (home)
- **2023** — CFP Championship
- **2026** — Venue World Cup
- **2028** — LA Olympics venue (opening & closing)

## Kapasitas

- **70.000** (NFL reguler)
- **100.000** (Super Bowl, konser)
- **Rekor**: 100.000+ (Super Bowl LVI)

## Fitur

- **Infinity Screen** — OLED 4K, 70.000 sq ft
- **Lantai translusent** — bisa lihat ke bawah
- **Atap** ETFE (khas, tembus pandang)

## Penggunaan

- **NFL**: LA Rams, LA Chargers
- **NCAA**: BCS, NCAA
- **2028 Olympics**: opening ceremony
- **Super Bowl**: 2022
