# Lumen Field

## Profil

**Lumen Field** di **Seattle, Washington**, AS. Home of **Seattle Seahawks** (NFL) dan **Seattle Sounders** (MLS). Kapasitas 69.000. Salah satu crowd paling berisik.

## Sejarah

- **2002** — Dibuka
- **2014** — Super Bowl XLVIII
- **2015** — Berganti nama CenturyLink
- **2019** — Berganti nama Lumen
- **2026** — Venue World Cup

## Kapasitas

- **69.000** (NFL)
- **72.000** (konser)

## Rekor Noise

- **137.6 dB** (2013, vs 49ers)
- **101.3 dB** (MLS, 2019) — MLS record

## Penggunaan

- **NFL**: Seattle Seahawks
- **MLS**: Seattle Sounders FC
- **XFL, USFL**: Seattle Sea Dragons
- **NCAA**: Washington Huskies
