# Mercedes-Benz Stadium

## Profil

**Mercedes-Benz Stadium** di **Atlanta, Georgia**, AS. Home of Atlanta Falcons (NFL) dan **Atlanta United** (MLS). Atap retracting (pinwheel camera-style) adalah fitur unik.

## Sejarah

- **2017** — Dibuka
- **2018** — Super Bowl LIII
- **2018** — College Football Playoff Championship
- **2019** — MLS Cup (Atlanta United)
- **2026** — Venue World Cup

## Kapasitas

- **71.000** (NFL)
- **75.000** (konser, MLS)
- **Bisa** sampai 83.000 (standing)

## Fitur

- **Halo Video Board** — 63.000 sq ft, 4K, terbesar di NFL
- **Atap** — pinwheel-style, membuka dalam <8 menit
- **Atap transparan** — bisa ditutup

## Penggunaan

- **NFL**: Atlanta Falcons
- **MLS**: Atlanta United
- **NCAA**: SEC Championship, Peach Bowl
- **Super Bowl**: LIII (2018)
