# Deep Learning

## Definisi

**Deep learning** (DL) adalah cabang [machine learning](/concepts/machine-learning) yang menggunakan [neural network](/concepts/neural-network) dengan banyak hidden layer (biasa 3-100+ layer).

Istilah "deep" merujuk pada kedalaman (jumlah layer) jaringan, bukan pada kedalaman pemahaman.

## Sejarah

- **1943** — McCulloch-Pitts model
- **1958** — [Perceptron](/concepts/perceptron) Rosenblatt
- **1986** — [Backpropagation](/concepts/backpropagation) populer (Rumelhart, Hinton, Williams)
- **1989** — LeNet ([Yann LeCun](/people/yann-lecun))
- **2006** — "Deep Learning" istilah diciptakan ([Hinton](/people/geoffrey-hinton))
- **2012** — AlexNet ([ImageNet](/timeline/imagenet-2012)) — revolusi dimulai
- **2018** — Turing Award untuk Hinton, LeCun, Bengio

## Arsitektur

- **[CNN](/concepts/convolutional-neural-network)** — gambar, video
- **[RNN](/concepts/recurrent-neural-network) / LSTM** — sequence, time series
- **[Transformer](/concepts/transformer)** — NLP, multimodal (2017–sekarang)
- **Diffusion models** — image generation
- **GAN** — generative adversarial

## Frameworks

PyTorch (Meta), TensorFlow (Google), JAX (Google), Keras.
