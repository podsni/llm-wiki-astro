# World Series of Poker (WSOP)

## Ringkasan

**World Series of Poker (WSOP)** adalah turnamen poker paling bergengsi di dunia. Dimulai 1970 di **Binion's Horseshoe** casino, Las Vegas, atas prakarsa **Benny Binion**. Main Event dimulai 1971.

## Sejarah

- **1970** — WSOP pertama, invitational, Johnny Moss juara
- **1971** — Main Event pertama (dengan buy-in)
- **1972** — Format freeze-out pertama
- **1979** — Stu Ungar juara pertama kali
- **2003** — **Moneymaker Effect** — Chris Moneymaker, $2.5M dari $86 online
- **2010s+** — Boom poker online
- **2023** — Main Event terbesar dalam sejarah (10.043 entries)
- **2024** — 55th anniversary WSOP

## Turnamen

- **Main Event** ($10.000 buy-in) — paling bergengsi
- **NLHE, PLO, Stud, HOSE, dll** — berbagai format
- **Seniors, Ladies, Online** — kategori khusus
- **Bracelets** — 80+ events per tahun
- **Circumvention event** — worldwide

## Pemenang Legendaris

- **Johnny Moss** (3x Main Event, 1970-1974)
- **Stu Ungar** (3x Main Event, 1980, 1981, 1997)
- **Doyle Brunson** (10x bracelet, 2x Main Event)
- **Phil Hellmuth** (17x bracelet — rekor)
- **Chris Moneymaker** (2003)
- **Daniel Negreanu** (6x bracelet)
- **Phil Ivey** (10x bracelet)
