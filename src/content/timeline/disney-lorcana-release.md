# Disney Lorcana Rilis

## Ringkasan

**Disney Lorcana** adalah trading card game yang dirilis pada **18 Agustus 2023** oleh **Ravensburger**. Karakter Disney (Mickey Mouse, Elsa, Stitch, dll) menjadi "glimmer" yang berjuang di **Realm of Lorcana**.

## Sejarah

- **2022** — Diumumkan di D23 Expo
- **2023-08-18** — The First Chapter (set 1) — 204 kartu
- **2023-12** — Rise of the Floodborn (set 2)
- **2024-03** — Into the Inklands (set 3)
- **2024-05** — Ursula's Return (set 4)
- **2024-08** — Shimmering Skies (set 5)
- **2024+** — Berlanjut

## Game

- **60-kartu deck** (minimum)
- **6 Ink colors**: Amber, Amethyst, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, Steel
- **Lore** — Illumineer melawan flood
- **Mechanic**: Songs, Actions, Items, Locations, Characters

## Tokoh

- **Ravensburger** — publisher
- **Ryan Miller** — co-lead designer
- **Joachim Knoop** — co-lead designer

## Karakter Ikonik

- **Mickey Mouse, Brave Little Tailor** (Amber)
- **Maleficent, Sinister Visitor** (Amethyst)
- **Stitch, Rock Star** (Ruby)
- **Elsa, Spirit of Winter** (Sapphire)
- **Donald Duck, Boisterous Fowl** (Emerald)
- **Captain Hook, Forceful Duelist** (Steel)

## Pasar

- **$200M+ revenue** (2023-2024)
- Lonjakan harga Mickey, Stitch Enchanted
- Mickey Mouse, Brave Little Tailor (Enchanted): $5K+
- Fast adoption di 2023
- **Booster pack** harga retail: $6-8
- **Tournament** Ravensburger League
